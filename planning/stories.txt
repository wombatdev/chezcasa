User Stories

As a user, I should be able to:

1) See food that my neighbors have for sale
2) Click on a picture of a dish to get more info
3) See the price and portion size of a dish I am interested in
4) Click a link to purchase that dish

As a chef, I should be able to:

1) Post dishes that I've cooked
2) Choose a price for my dish
3) Edit my dish to update information
4) Delete a dish I have posted
